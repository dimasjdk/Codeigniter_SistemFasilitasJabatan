Frequently Asked Questions

How can I pay?

We accept PayPal, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover cards. We do not store payment/card information in our server. After your payment has been completed, log into the member area to download themes.

Why choose Premium Theme?

We have been building WordPress Themes for 7 years Our designs are beautiful, functional and affordable. With dozens of themes for $49, you are getting access to one of the largest collections of premium themes available for half the price that our competitors pay for a single theme!

Can I use the themes on multiple websites?

Yes, you are free to use our themes on as many websites as you like. We do not place any restrictions on how many times you can download or use a theme, nor do we limit the number of domains that you can install our themes to.

Can I use Themes for my clients?

Yes, you are free to use our themes to build websites for your clients. We love providing developers with beautiful and functional design choices for their clients!

What if I change my mind and want a refund?

We are offering a partial or full refund within 14 business days starting from the moment of successful payment. Before purchase please read Refund Policy or Contact Us if you need further information.

Can I upgrade to a different package later?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time after signing up. When you log in as a "personal" subscriber, you will see a notice regarding your current package and instructions on how to upgrade.

Can I modify Themes and remove the footer links?

Yes, you can modify them as much as you like. You can completely remove all links using theme settings and insert your content (link, logo) instead. But don't remove Premium Theme copyright code from XML and PHP files.

Can I still use the themes after my membership expires?

Yes, you can use templates according to terms, but you won't have access to updates and download section. But you can renew membership anytime.

Can I share my username and password with others?

No. A username and password is specific to one person with up to five IP addresses only. If you share your details you will receive an automatic suspension of your account. If your download traffic logs exceed the total number of allowed downloads, your account will be suspended immediately.

Can I buy a single theme instead of joining the membership?

Our themes are only available as part of the membership. However, I think you will find that $49 is an incredible deal, even if you only intend to use a single theme from our collection. $49 is less than the price you would pay for a single theme at many other theme shops, and here you get access to a lot more!

Will your themes work with third party plugins?

While our themes should work fine with most plugins, there is no way for us to test and guarantee that all plugins will work. All we can guarantee is that our themes are coded excellently and that any plugin that also uses coding best practices should play well with our themes. If you run into any problems, let us know so that we can look into it.

Do your subscriptions auto-renew every year?

Our subscriptions do not auto-renew. Each payment is a one off payment and you can continue to use the Premium WordPress Themes on your site after your subscription has expired.
